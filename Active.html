Denmark

Denmark is a great country in Europe. Nature is
beautiful, people are happy, and food is tasty!
Living in Denmark for a summer I realize how
amazing this place is, and that one day I would
like to live here. People here are active and
healthy. Copenhagen is very popular tourist spot during the summer. And why not? This place have everything. However, Denmark is not only known as the happiest people land, but also one of the most expensive places in Europe. But it is still worth to go there!

Moscow

A place where traditions and historical places
meets. Cities such as Moscow and Saint-Petersburg will break stereotypes and change your thoughts about Russia. Moscow is very modern city with skycrapers and new technologies, however, it still holds her traditions with ortodox churces and ornaments. Russian cuisine is very simple- borsch, dumplings, crepes, etc., but the taste is very rich. Saint-Petersburg is one of my favorite cities becasue it is traditional and beautiful in a special way. Saint-Petersburg is must visit city in the Russia.
