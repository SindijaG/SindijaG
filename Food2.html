It's Pizza Time!

Post by Sindija G., Oct 15, 2019.
FoodCheat Meal

Homemade pizza for cheat day? Yes! Make your dough from flour, water and yeast, then let it rise in warm place. After an hour roll the dough and put all ingredients on the top. My secret for perfect pizza is LOTS OF CHEESE and greens. Isn't is easy?
