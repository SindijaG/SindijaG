Happiness is Homemade

Best Breakfast Choice

I start my day with oats! Easy to make and very
tasty. You can add everything what your heart
desires. I chose berries, chia and pumpkin
seeds, and banana. Gives me energy for the day! And of course great breakfast option while you are travelling.

It's Pizza Time!

Homemade pizza for cheat day? Yes! Make your
dough from flour, water and yeast, then let
it rise in warm place. After an hour roll the
dough and put all ingredients on the top. My
secret for perfect pizza is LOTS OF CHEESE and greens. Isn't is easy?

Makin' Bakin' Eatin'

Sweets are my weakness. Great alternative for
cookies and chocolates are these little cinamon
rolls. Frozen dough, little bit of sugar and lot
of cinamon and pumpkin spice. This definitely is a cheaper version of American cinamon rolls.
